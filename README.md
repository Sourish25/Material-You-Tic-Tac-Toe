# Material You Tic Tac Toe

A stunning, feature-rich logic puzzle game built with modern Android technologies. **Material You Tic Tac Toe** reimagines the classic pen-and-paper game with dynamic theming, intelligent AI, and over 10 unique game modes that challenge your strategy in new ways.

> [!NOTE]
> Designed with **Jetpack Compose** and **Material 3** for a buttery smooth, adaptive UI that matches your system wallpaper.

## ‚ú® Features

- **Material You Design**: The UI adapts to your device's wallpaper and theme colors (Dynamic Color).
- **Single Player & Multiplayer**: Play against a friend locally or challenge the AI.
- **Smart AI**: Features an Adaptive AI with multiple difficulty levels (Easy, Hard) capable of playing all game modes.
- **Immersive Experience**: Includes sound effects, haptic feedback, and confetti celebrations.
- **Completely Ad-Free & Open Source**.

## üéÆ Game Modes

This isn't just your grandmother's Tic Tac Toe. Explore **10 distinct game modes**:

1.  **Classic**: The traditional 3x3 grid you know and love.
2.  **Ultimate**: The complex 9x9 nested strategy game. Win local grids to capture the cell in the global board.
3.  **Gravity**: A "Connect 4" style twist where pieces fall to the lowest available slot in the column.
4.  **Fog of War**: The board is hidden! Cells are revealed only when you or your opponent plays near them.
5.  **Bomb**: Watch out! 15% chance for a move to explode, clearing surrounding cells.
6.  **Speed**: Think fast! A countdown timer forces quick decisions. Run out of time and lose your turn.
7.  **Blocked**: Start the game with random cells already "dead" and unplayable.
8.  **Chaos**: 10% chance that your piece will flip to your opponent's symbol when placed. Pure anarchy.
9.  **Fading**: Old moves fade away after a few turns, constantly changing the board state.
10. **Misere**: The goal is to **lose**. Force your opponent to make a line of 3 to win.
